Adventures and Travels

Something else that I wanted to highlight on this site that is a huge part of my life are the travels that I have been fortunate enough to have been on! Ever since I was a child, my parents and family friends have toted me along to the craziest of places, from Alaska, to South Africa, to Thailand.

When I travel, I love to take the opportunity to enjoy nature and appreciate the culture of the people I encounter.

Below are a few examples of places and experiences I have had in the last few years.

Mission Trip

During my freshman year of college, I was given the opportunity to travel to the Dominican Republic through an organization called Global Medical Training as part of a medical mission trip. I really enjoyed this experience because it combined my passions for medicine and health with my desire to understand more about people living in different places.
